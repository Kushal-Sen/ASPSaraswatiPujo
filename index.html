Saraswati Puja Committee

Inaugural Saraswati Puja of Aparna Serene Park 2021

सरस्वती महाभागे विद्ये कमललोचने ।
विद्यारूपे विशालाक्षि विद्यां देहि नमोस्तुते ।।
या देवी सर्वभूतेषु विद्या रूपेण संस्थिता ।
नमस्तस्यै नमस्तस्यै नमस्तस्यै नमो नम: ।।

Saraswati is the goddess of knowledge, music, art, speech, wisdom, and learning. This auspicious occasion is celebrated on Vasant Panchami or the fifth day of spring. We request all the residents of the community to come and seek blessings from the goddess of wisdom and be a part of the very first Saraswati Pujo of our residential complex "Aparna Serene Park".
